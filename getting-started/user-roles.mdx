Every person who has a Neuramill account is assigned one of three roles. Your role controls what you can see and do inside the platform.

## Three Roles at a Glance

| Role | Who This Usually Is |
|------|---------------------|
| **Super Admin** | The person who set up the company account. Full control of everything. |
| **Admin** | A manager or lead who can invite team members and manage machines, but cannot manage billing. |
| **User** | A machinist or operator who creates and manages their own jobs. Cannot invite others or manage the account. |

## Full Permissions Table

| Action | Super Admin | Admin | User |
|--------|------------|-------|------|
| Create machining sessions (jobs) | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |
| View their own sessions | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |
| View all company sessions | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ |
| Invite new Admins | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ |
| Invite new Users | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ |
| Delete a User | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ |
| Delete an Admin | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ |
| Delete a Super Admin | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ |
| Add machines | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ |
| Manage tool library | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ |
| Manage billing and subscription | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ |

<Note>
Nobody can delete a Super Admin from within the application. Contact the Neuramill team if a Super Admin account needs to be removed.
</Note>

## How Roles Are Assigned

- The **first person** to register a company account automatically becomes the Super Admin.
- Super Admins and Admins can **send email invitations** to new team members and choose their role (Admin or User) when sending the invite.
- A regular **User cannot change their own role**. Only an Admin or Super Admin can do that.

## If You Are a Regular User

If you log in and see that you cannot access the Users tab or the Machines tab — that is expected. Regular Users only see and manage their own jobs. To get Admin access, ask your Super Admin to update your role.
