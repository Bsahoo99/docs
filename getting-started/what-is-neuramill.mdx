Neuramill is a computer program that reads your part's 3D CAD file and builds a complete machining plan for you.

You have been machining parts for decades. You know how long it takes to sit down with a drawing, figure out every feature, look up speeds and feeds from charts, and write it all down. That part — the lookup and calculation work — is what Neuramill handles.

**You upload your CAD file. Neuramill reads it and tells you:**

- Every feature that needs to be machined — every hole, pocket, flat surface, and contour
- Which operations each feature needs (drilling, milling, reaming, facing, and so on)
- How to clamp and hold the part for each setup
- Which cutting tools to use
- The speeds and feeds to run those tools

![Neuramill upload dialog with CAD and GD&T drop zones](/images/onboarding/screenshots/s01-what-is-neuramill-upload-overview.webp)

<Note>
**Neuramill does not replace your judgment.** It handles the lookup and calculation work. You review everything it suggests and give your approval before the plan is finalized. Nothing is done without your say-so.
</Note>

## How the Workflow Goes

Every session in Neuramill follows the same path from left to right. **You cannot skip ahead** — each step must be finished before the next one unlocks.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload Your Part">
    Provide your STEP file. If you have a GD&T drawing, add that too.
    Neuramill reads the 3D geometry and finds every machinable feature automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 1 — Direction of Cut">
    Review every feature the AI found. Check that each one has the right operation and the right approach direction. Fix anything that looks wrong.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2 — Machining Setup">
    Tell Neuramill what the part is made of, which machine you will use, how stiff your setup is, and the size of your raw stock block.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 3 — Clamping Strategy">
    Define how the part will be held for each setup. Make sure every feature can be reached by the cutting tool.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 4 — Intelligent Tool Recommender">
    Review the recommended cutting tools and speeds/feeds for each operation. Override anything that does not suit your shop.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 5 — Summary">
    Review the completed plan. Everything is saved on the Neuramill server.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Neuramill runs entirely in your **web browser**. You do not need to install any software on your computer. All you need is an internet connection and a browser — Chrome works best.
</Note>
