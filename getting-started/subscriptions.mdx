Neuramill has two subscription plans: Trial and Enterprise.

## Two plans

| Plan | Who It Is For | How You Get It |
|------|---------------|----------------|
| **Trial** | New users evaluating Neuramill | Sign up without a license key |
| **Enterprise** | Production shops with full access | Sign up with a 16-character license key from the Neuramill team, or be invited by your company admin |

## Trial plan

On the Trial plan you can work through the full workflow. Tool recommendations come from the **Neuramill general catalog** rather than your own shop inventory, because a Trial account does not have a private tool library set up yet.

The Trial lasts **28 days**. After it ends, contact the Neuramill team to move to an Enterprise plan.

## Enterprise plan

Enterprise accounts use your company's **own tool library**, and you see a green **Owned** badge next to tools already in your shop's inventory.

## Where to see your current plan

1. Look at the top-right corner of any Neuramill screen.
2. You will see a small circle with your initials. Click it.
3. Click **My Profile** from the menu that appears.
4. Your subscription plan is shown in the **Account Information** section.
