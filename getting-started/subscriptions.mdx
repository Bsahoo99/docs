Neuramill has two subscription plans. Your plan controls how many jobs, users, and machines your company can use.

## Two Plans

| Plan | Who It Is For | How You Get It |
|------|---------------|----------------|
| **Trial** | New users evaluating Neuramill | Sign up without a license key |
| **Enterprise** | Production shops with full access | Sign up with a 16-character license key from the Neuramill team, or be invited by your company admin |

## Trial Plan — What Is Included and What Is Limited

The Trial plan has hard limits. Once you hit a limit, the relevant button turns grey and cannot be clicked.

| Resource | Trial Limit |
|----------|-------------|
| Sessions (jobs) | **5 total** |
| Users | **1 user account** |
| Machines | **2 machines** |

**What happens at each limit:**

- **Sessions:** Once you have 5, the **+ New Session** button turns grey. A message appears: *"Job limit reached."* You cannot start another session until you upgrade.
- **Users:** The **Invite User** button turns grey. No one else can be added to your account.
- **Machines:** If you try to add a third machine, the system shows: *"Upgrade your plan to add more machines."*

<Warning>
The Trial plan also has an **expiration date**. Even if you have not used all 5 sessions, once the trial period ends you will not be able to start new sessions. Check your expiration date on the My Profile page.
</Warning>

**Tool library on Trial:**
Because Trial accounts do not have a private tool library set up yet, the Tool Recommender step shows tools from the Neuramill general catalog rather than your own shop's inventory.

## Enterprise Plan — No Limits

| Resource | Enterprise |
|----------|-----------|
| Sessions | Unlimited |
| Users | Unlimited |
| Machines | Unlimited |
| Tool library | Your company's own inventory |

Enterprise users also see a green **Owned** badge next to tools that are already in their shop's inventory.

<Tip>
If you see the word **"Unlimited"** next to Sessions, Users, or Machines on your Profile page, you are on the Enterprise plan.
</Tip>

## Where to See Your Current Plan

1. Look at the top-right corner of any Neuramill screen.
2. You will see a small circle with your initials. Click it.
3. Click **My Profile** from the menu that appears.
4. Your subscription plan is shown in the **Account Information** section.
