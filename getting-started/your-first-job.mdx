This page is a map of one complete session. Each step links to the detailed page where you can learn more. Follow it once and you will know the whole workflow.

<Note>
Before you begin, make sure your CNC machine is set up in your [Machines library](/dashboard/machines-tab) — you choose it in Step 1, so it must already be there. If it is not listed, ask an admin to add it.
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in">
    Open Neuramill and sign in. You land on the Dashboard, where your jobs are listed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start a new session and upload your CAD">
    Click **+ New Session** and upload your `STEP` file. You can also upload a GD&T drawing alongside it for tighter tolerance matching. See [Understand sessions](/starting-a-session/what-is-a-session).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and approve GD&T">
    If you uploaded a drawing, review the extracted annotations and approve them. This step unlocks Step 2. See [GD&T review](/gdt-review/overview).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 1 — Machining setup">
    Set your material, machine, rigidity, and stock size, and approve the extracted features. See [Configure machining setup](/machining-setup/overview).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2 — Direction of cut">
    Review each feature's classification and machining direction, then approve. See [Direction of cut](/direction-of-cut/overview).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 3 — Clamping strategy">
    Define how the part is held in each setup so every feature is reachable. See [Understand clamping strategy](/clamping-strategy/what-is-clamping).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 4 — Intelligent tool recommender">
    Get tool recommendations with speeds and feeds for every operation. See [Intelligent tool recommender](/tool-recommender/overview).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 5 — Summary">
    Review the complete plan in one read-only view before you finalize it. See [Review the session summary](/summary/summary-view).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export to Fusion 360">
    Send the finished plan to the Neuramill add-in inside Fusion 360. See [Fusion 360 add-in](/fusion-addin/overview).
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
The steps run in order and each must be approved before the next unlocks. You can always go back to an earlier step, change something, and re-approve.
</Note>
