---
title: "Upload a CAD file"
description: "How to start a session with just a STEP file and no GD&T drawing."
---
Choose this option if you have a STEP file but do not have an engineering drawing — or if you want to proceed without one.

<Warning>
**This is a one-way decision.** Once you click **Upload CAD Only**, you **cannot** add a GD&T drawing to this session later. If you later find your drawing and want to link tolerances, you will need to start a brand new session. Take a few extra minutes now to locate your drawing before uploading — it can save you from starting over.
</Warning>

## Step-by-step instructions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click + New Session on the Dashboard">
    From the Dashboard, click the **+ New Session** button in the top-right corner. The upload page opens.
    
    ![Upload page with two upload zones for CAD and GD&T](/images/onboarding/screenshots/s01-what-is-neuramill-upload-overview.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find your STEP file on your computer">
    Your STEP file has the extension `.step` or `.stp` at the end of its name. It is usually in a folder called something like "Parts," "Exports," or "CAD files."
    
    If you are not sure where it is, ask the person who made the part drawing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload your STEP file to the left zone">
    The **left upload zone** on the upload page is for your CAD file.
    
    **Option A — Drag and drop:**
    - Open the folder where your STEP file is saved.
    - Click and hold on the file.
    - Drag it onto the left upload zone in your browser.
    - Release your mouse button.
    
    **Option B — Click to browse:**
    - Click anywhere inside the left upload zone.
    - A file browser window opens.
    - Navigate to your STEP file and double-click it.
    
    ![Upload page with CAD file attached and green checkmark](/images/onboarding/screenshots/s18-upload-cad-only-step-attached.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the file was accepted">
    When the file is accepted, you will see the **filename** and a **green checkmark** inside the upload zone. This means it worked.
    
    If you see a red error instead, the file may be the wrong format. Only `.step` and `.stp` files are accepted.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Go to Tool Recommender">
    A blue button appears below the upload zone. It says **Upload CAD Only**. Click it.
    
    Neuramill now reads your file and finds every machinable feature. This usually takes **10 to 30 seconds**. A spinning indicator appears — do not close the browser tab while this is running.
  </Step>
</Steps>
