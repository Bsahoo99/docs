A **session** in Neuramill is one complete machining plan for one part.

Every time you want to plan a new part — or a new version of a part you have done before — you start a **new session**.

## Key facts about sessions

- **One session = one STEP file.** You cannot have multiple parts in one session.
- **Sessions are saved automatically.** If you close your browser and come back later, your session is still there — find it in the Jobs tab on the Dashboard.
- **Sessions move through 5 steps in order.** You cannot skip ahead. Each step must be finished before the next one unlocks.
- **You can go back and change things.** If you made a mistake in an earlier step, you can go back, fix it, and re-approve.

## 5 steps of every session

| Step | Name | What Happens |
|------|------|-------------|
| 1 | Machining Setup | Set material, machine, rigidity, and stock size; confirm the extracted features. |
| 2 | Direction of Cut | Review every feature the AI detected. Confirm operations and directions. |
| 3 | Clamping Strategy | Define how the part will be held. Make sure every feature is accessible. |
| 4 | Intelligent Tool Recommender | Get tool recommendations and speeds/feeds. |
| 5 | Summary | Review the complete plan. |

## Starting a new session

From the Dashboard, click the **+ New Session** button in the top-right corner. The upload page opens.
