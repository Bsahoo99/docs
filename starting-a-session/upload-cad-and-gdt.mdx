---
title: "Upload CAD and GD&T"
description: "How to start a session with both a STEP file and an engineering drawing."
---
Choose this option if you have both a STEP file and a 2D engineering drawing with tolerances (GD&T). Uploading both gives Neuramill more information and produces more accurate tool recommendations for tight-tolerance features.

## Step-by-step instructions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click + New Session on the Dashboard">
    From the Dashboard, click the **+ New Session** button. The upload page opens.
    You will see two upload zones side by side — the left is for CAD, the right is for GD&T.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload your STEP file to the LEFT zone">
    Follow the same steps as in [Upload CAD Only](/starting-a-session/upload-cad-only) to attach your `.step` or `.stp` file to the **left** upload zone.
    
    Wait for the green checkmark and filename to appear before continuing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload your drawing to the RIGHT zone">
    The **right upload zone** is for your GD&T drawing.
    
    Accepted formats: `.pdf`, `.tiff`, or `.tif`
    
    Drag and drop the drawing file onto the right zone, or click inside it to browse for the file.
    
    ![Upload page with CAD and GD&T files attached and validated](/images/onboarding/screenshots/s19-upload-cad-gdt-both-attached.webp)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm both zones show a green checkmark">
    Both the left (CAD) and right (GD&T) zones should show a filename and a green checkmark. If either one is missing the checkmark, that file was not accepted — check the file format and try again.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click View & Analyze">
    A green button appears: **View & Analyze** (or **Upload CAD + GD&T**). Click it.
    
    Neuramill processes both files. This usually takes 10 to 30 seconds. A spinning indicator appears — do not close the browser tab while this is running.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Next steps

After processing, you are taken to the **GD&T review** screen first, where you review the extracted annotations and link them to 3D features. Once you approve the GD&T, you proceed into the studio, starting with **Step 1 — Machining setup**.

<Note>
If you uploaded a GD&T drawing, you will see an extra step — [GD&T review](/gdt-review/overview) — before the studio steps. This screen lets you connect tolerance callouts on the drawing to specific features on the 3D model. Approving it unlocks **Step 2 — Direction of cut**.
</Note>
