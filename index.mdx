Neuramill automates CNC process planning using AI. Upload your CAD model and GD&T drawing, and Neuramill handles tool selection, clamping strategy, machining policies, and speeds & feeds — in minutes, not days.

## Five-step workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Direction of cut">
    Review how Neuramill classifies each feature and assign the machining direction. [Go to Step 1 →](/direction-of-cut/overview)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Machining setup">
    Set the material, machine, rigidity, and stock size for your part. [Go to Step 2 →](/machining-setup/overview)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clamping strategy">
    Define how the part is held in each setup and distribute features across clampings. [Go to Step 3 →](/clamping-strategy/what-is-clamping)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tool recommender">
    Review AI-recommended tools, speeds, and feeds for every operation. [Go to Step 4 →](/tool-recommender/overview)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Summary">
    Inspect the final process plan and export or finalize your session. [Go to Step 5 →](/summary/summary-view)
  </Step>
</Steps>
