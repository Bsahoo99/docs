Neuramill automates CNC process planning using AI. Upload your CAD model and GD&T drawing, and Neuramill handles tool selection, clamping strategy, machining policies, and speeds & feeds — in minutes, not days.

## Five-step workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Machining setup">
    Set the material, machine, rigidity, and stock size for your part, and confirm the extracted features. [Go to Step 1 →](/machining-setup/overview)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Direction of cut">
    Review how Neuramill classifies each feature and confirm the machining direction. [Go to Step 2 →](/direction-of-cut/overview)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clamping strategy">
    Define how the part is held in each setup and distribute features across clampings. [Go to Step 3 →](/clamping-strategy/what-is-clamping)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Intelligent tool recommender">
    Review AI-recommended tools, speeds, and feeds for every operation. [Go to Step 4 →](/tool-recommender/overview)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Summary">
    Inspect the final process plan, review cycle time, and finalize your session. [Go to Step 5 →](/summary/summary-view)
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Uploaded a GD&T drawing? You review and link annotations in [GD&T review](/gdt-review/overview) before Step 2. When your plan is ready, [export it to Fusion 360](/fusion-addin/overview).
</Note>
