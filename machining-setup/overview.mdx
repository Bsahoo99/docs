Machining Setup is where you tell Neuramill four things about the physical job:

1. **What is the part made of?** (Material)
2. **Which machine are you using?** (Machine)
3. **How stiff is your setup?** (Rigidity)
4. **How big is your raw stock block?** (Stock Size)

These four inputs drive every cutting parameter calculation that follows. Getting them right here means better speeds, feeds, and tool recommendations in Step 4.

## Approving features first

At the very top of the Machining Setup panel, you will see an **Extracted Features** section. This is your last chance to review the features before they get locked in for the rest of the session.

See [Approving Features](/machining-setup/approving-features) for how to do this.
