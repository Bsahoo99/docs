title: "Validate stock and approve setup"
description: "After entering stock dimensions, Neuramill validates them. Look for the green banner before approving."

After you enter stock dimensions, Neuramill checks whether the stock is large enough to contain the part and fits within the machine's work envelope.

## Validation result

| Result | What You See | What to Do |
|--------|-------------|------------|
| **✓ Stock Validated** | Green banner: *"Stock is valid for this machine and CAD part."* | You are ready to approve and move forward. |
| **Stock Too Small** | Red or orange validation error banner | Increase one or more of the X, Y, or Z dimensions. Keep adjusting until the error disappears and the green banner appears. |
| **Warning** | Yellow notice | Stock is technically large enough but tight. Review carefully before proceeding. |

![Stock validated success banner](/images/onboarding/screenshots/s35-machining-stock-validated-banner.webp)

<Warning>
Do not click the **Machining Setup Approved** button until you see the green "Stock Validated" banner. Proceeding with invalid stock will cause errors in later steps.
</Warning>

## Approving machining setup

Once all four fields are set (Material, Machine, Rigidity, Stock) and the stock shows as validated:

1. Click the **Machining Setup Approved** button at the very bottom of the right panel.
2. The button changes to a green **✓ Approved** badge.
3. The **Direction of cut** step unlocks in the navigation bar.

![Machining Setup Approved button before clicking (blue) and after clicking (green Approved badge)](/images/onboarding/screenshots/s36-machining-setup-approved-before-after.webp)
