**Quick setup** is an express path on the **Step 1 — Machining setup** screen, found just below the **Validate Machining Setup** button. Instead of reviewing every step yourself, you let Neuramill make the default choices for the remaining steps and jump ahead, so you reach tool recommendations faster.

## What it does

When you choose quick setup, a confirmation explains what will be skipped before you commit. Neuramill then makes the default choices for those steps on your behalf and takes you forward to the tool recommendations.

## When to use it

Quick setup trades manual control for speed. It works best when you trust the defaults or want a fast first pass.

<Warning>
Quick setup auto-decides steps you would normally review yourself. Review the result before you export it.
</Warning>

<Note>
Choosing quick setup does not lock anything in. You can still go back to any auto-decided step, adjust it, and re-approve.
</Note>
