There are two ways to get a Neuramill account.

## Way 1 — you received an invitation email

Your company admin sent you an invitation link by email.

1. Open your email inbox and find the email from Neuramill.
2. Click the link inside the email. It takes you to a registration page where your email is already filled in.
3. Set a password and click **Register**.
4. You are taken to the Dashboard automatically.

<Note>
The invitation link expires after 48 hours. If the link does not work, ask your admin to resend it from the Invitations tab on the Dashboard.
</Note>

## Way 2 — signing up without an invitation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your browser">
    Open your browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge — all major modern browsers are supported).
    Go to [https://app.neuramill.co](https://app.neuramill.co/).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Register">
    On the sign-in page, click **Register** or the text that says *"Don't have an account? Sign up."*
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in the form">
    Fill in every field in the registration form. See the table below for what to type in each field.
    
    ![Registration form with all fields labeled](/images/onboarding/screenshots/s04-account-registration-form-labeled.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Register">
    Click the **Register** button at the bottom of the form.
    If everything is correct, you are taken to the Dashboard automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Registration form fields

| Field | What to Type |
|-------|-------------|
| First Name | Your first name |
| Last Name | Your last name |
| Email | Your work email address |
| Phone Number | Your phone number |
| Password | A new password — see password rules below |
| Confirm Password | Type the same password again exactly |
| Company Name | Your company's name |
| License Key | Leave blank unless you have an Enterprise key from Neuramill |

## Password rules

Your password must meet **all five** of these requirements or the form will show a red error.

| Requirement | Example |
|-------------|---------|
| At least 8 characters long | `S7r!ngP@ss9` has 11 characters |
| At least one UPPERCASE letter | The `S` and `P` in `S7r!ngP@ss9` |
| At least one lowercase letter | The `r`, `ng`, and `ss` in `S7r!ngP@ss9` |
| At least one number | The `7` and `9` in `S7r!ngP@ss9` |
| At least one special character | The `!` and `@` in `S7r!ngP@ss9` |

<Warning>
If you see a red error message after clicking Register, read it carefully. It will tell you exactly which field has a problem. Fix that field and try again.
</Warning>
