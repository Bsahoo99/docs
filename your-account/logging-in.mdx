---
title: "Sign in"
description: "How to log in to Neuramill every time you use it."
---
<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your browser and go to Neuramill">
    Open **Chrome** on your computer.
    Go to [https://app.neuramill.co](https://app.neuramill.co/) in the address bar at the very top of the browser window and press **Enter** on your keyboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You see the Login page">
    The Login page has two fields: one for your email address and one for your password.
    
    ![Login page with Email Password and Login button labeled](/images/onboarding/screenshots/s05-login-form-labeled.webp)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Type your email address">
    Click inside the **Email** field. A blinking line (cursor) appears inside it.
    Type your work email address — the same one you used when you registered.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Type your password">
    Click inside the **Password** field and type your password.
    
    If you want to see what you are typing, click the small **eye icon** on the right side of the password field. Click it again to hide it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Log In">
    Click the **Log In** button.
    You are taken to your Dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## If the Login Does Not Work

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="The page says: Invalid email or password">
    One of the two fields was typed incorrectly.
    
    - Check that **Caps Lock is not on**. The Caps Lock key is on the left side of the keyboard above the Shift key. If the indicator light next to it is lit, press it once to turn Caps Lock off.
    - Passwords are case-sensitive — `millwork1` and `Millwork1` are treated as different passwords.
    - Try typing your password slowly, one character at a time.
    - Still not working? See [Forgot Your Password](/your-account/forgot-password).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="The page is not loading">
    Check your internet connection by trying to open another website (like google.com). If other sites load but Neuramill does not, contact your admin or the Neuramill support team.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I see a blank white screen after logging in">
    Press the **F5** key on your keyboard to refresh the page. Or click the circular arrow near the top of the browser window.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
