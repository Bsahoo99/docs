---
title: "Reset your password"
description: "How to reset your Neuramill password."
---
<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the log in page">
    Open your browser and go to [https://app.neuramill.co](https://app.neuramill.co/). You see the log in page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Forgot Password?">
    Look for the link that says **Forgot Password?** — it is below the password field or below the **Log In** button. Click it.
    
    {/* TODO: Add screenshot — Forgot Password link highlighted on the log in page */}
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your email address">
    A new page appears with one field: your email address.
    Type the email you registered with and click **Send Reset Link**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your email inbox">
    You will receive an email from Neuramill with a reset link. It may take a few minutes to arrive. If you do not see it, check your **Spam** or **Junk** folder.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click the link in the email">
    Open the email and click the reset link. It takes you to a page where you can create a new password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a new password">
    Type your new password in both fields (you must type it twice to confirm). Follow the same password rules: at least 8 characters, one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, one number.
    
    Click **Reset Password**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log in with your new password">
    Go back to the log in page and log in with your new password.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Password reset links **expire after 24 hours**. If the link shows an error, go back and click "Forgot Password?" again to get a fresh link.
</Warning>
