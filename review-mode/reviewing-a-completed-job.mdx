**Review mode** is a read-only view of a completed session. Use it to inspect or share a finished plan without changing it — for design review, collaboration, and archival.

![Review mode with locked step tabs and the Summary visible](/images/onboarding/screenshots/s67-review-mode-locked-tabs.png)

## Open a session in review mode

You open review mode from the Dashboard Jobs list by opening a completed or closed job.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Jobs list">
    Open the **Jobs** tab on the Dashboard. See [Manage jobs](/dashboard/jobs-tab).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open a completed job">
    Open a job that is completed or closed. It opens in review mode.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What you can do

All five step tabs are visible but locked, so you can move through the plan without editing it. The two things you will usually do here are review the Summary and generate a Fusion export key.

| Area | What you can do |
|------|-----------------|
| Step 1 Machining setup | View, read-only. |
| Step 2 Direction of cut | View, read-only. |
| Step 3 Clamping strategy | View, read-only. |
| Step 4 Intelligent tool recommender | View, read-only. |
| Step 5 Summary | View the finished plan, and generate a Fusion export key. See [Generating an export key](/fusion-addin/generating-an-export-key). |
| GD&T annotations | View-only. |
| NeuraChat | Ask questions about the session. See [Use NeuraChat](/neurachat/using-neurachat). |

<Note>
Review mode does not change the session. To edit a plan, open it as an active session instead.
</Note>
