---
When you have reviewed all features — confirmed directions, checked operations, and flagged anything wrong — you are ready to approve this step and move forward.

## Before You Approve — Quick Checklist

<Check>All features in the feature list have been reviewed</Check>
<Check>Each feature is assigned to the correct face direction (Top, Bottom, Front, Back, Left, Right)</Check>
<Check>Any features with wrong dimensions have been flagged and corrected</Check>
<Check>Any features that should not be machined have been flagged as Extra Feature</Check>
<Check>The Operations dropdown on each feature card has been checked — the assigned operations make sense</Check>

## Approve

1. Click the **Direction of Cut Approved** button at the bottom of the right panel.
2. The button changes to a green **✓ Approved** badge.
3. The **Machining Setup** step unlocks and becomes clickable in the navigation bar at the top.

![Direction of Cut screen with approve control visible](/images/onboarding/screenshots/s21-direction-of-cut-overview-tabs-approve.webp)

## If You Need to Come Back

After approving, you can still go back to this step at any time. Click **Direction of Cut** in the navigation bar at the top of the screen. Make your changes. Then click Approve again to re-lock it.

<Note>
Going back and making changes in Direction of Cut may require you to re-approve steps that come after it.
</Note>
