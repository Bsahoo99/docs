---
title: "Change feature setup direction"
description: "How to move a feature from one face direction to another when the AI assigned it to the wrong face."
---
The AI assigns each feature to a direction based on the part geometry. It is usually right — but not always. If a feature is assigned to the wrong face, or if you prefer to approach it from a different direction, you can change it.

## Change a feature's direction

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the feature card for that feature">
    In Hybrid view or Grid view — find the feature card for the feature you want to change.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click on the feature card">
    Left-click anywhere on the feature card — not on the flag icon or the operations dropdown arrow. Just click the card itself.
    
    A small popup menu appears, labeled **MOVE TO SETUP**.
    
    ![Move to setup popup with six direction options](/images/onboarding/screenshots/s26-direction-move-to-setup-popup.webp)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the current direction">
    The direction currently assigned has a **solid filled black dot** (●) next to it. The others have open circles.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click your new direction">
    Click the colored dot next to the direction you want to assign this feature to.
    
    The feature card's direction badge updates immediately to the new direction and color.
    The 3D model also updates to reflect the change.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
If you click the wrong direction by accident, just click the feature card again and correct it. **No Save button is needed** — changes happen immediately and are stored automatically.
</Tip>

## When would you change a direction?

| Situation | What to Do |
|-----------|-----------|
| The AI put a top-face hole under "Front" | Click the feature card → MOVE TO SETUP → Top |
| You want to drill all holes from the Top even if some are on the side | Reassign side holes to Top |
| You are flipping your clamping strategy and want all bottom features together | Move bottom features to Bottom |
