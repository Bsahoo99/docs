This is the second step in your session, after you approve the machining setup. It is your quality check of the AI's interpretation of your part.

**What happens in this step:**
- Neuramill has already read your STEP file and found every machinable feature: holes, pockets, flat faces, slots, contours, and more
- It has also assigned each feature to a machining direction: Top, Bottom, Front, Back, Left, or Right
- Your job is to **review** every feature, check that the direction and operations look right, and **correct anything that is wrong**

<Note>
The AI is very good at reading geometry — but it is not perfect. You are the expert on your part. This step is where your machining knowledge matters most. Take your time here. Mistakes in Step 2 carry through to wrong tool recommendations in Step 4.
</Note>

## Two views

You can switch between two views:

| View | What It Shows | Best Used For |
|------|--------------|---------------|
| **Hybrid View** | The 3D model alongside feature cards grouped by direction | The default — see a feature on the part and review its details together |
| **Grid View** | All features in a flat table | Scanning through everything at once |

Both views show the **same information** — just organized differently. Use whichever helps you review most clearly.
