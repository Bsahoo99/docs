This is the first step inside your session after your file has been processed. It is your quality check of the AI's interpretation of your part.

**What happens in this step:**
- Neuramill has already read your STEP file and found every machinable feature: holes, pockets, flat faces, slots, contours, and more
- It has also assigned each feature to a machining direction: Top, Bottom, Front, Back, Left, or Right
- Your job is to **review** every feature, check that the direction and operations look right, and **correct anything that is wrong**

<Note>
The AI is very good at reading geometry — but it is not perfect. You are the expert on your part. This step is where your machining knowledge matters most. Take your time here. Mistakes in Step 1 carry through to wrong tool recommendations in Step 4.
</Note>

## Three Views

The panel on the right side of the screen has three tabs you can switch between:

| Tab | What It Shows | Best Used For |
|-----|--------------|---------------|
| **CAD View** | Six face buttons + Analytics panel | Quick overview — see how many features are on each face |
| **Setup View** | Feature cards grouped by direction | Checking that features are in the right groups |
| **Grid View** | All features in a flat table | Scanning through everything at once |

All three views show the **same information** — just organized differently. Use whichever helps you review most clearly.
