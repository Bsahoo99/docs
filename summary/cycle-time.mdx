On the [Summary step](/summary/summary-view), Neuramill shows an estimated **cycle time** for the plan. Use it to gauge how long the job will take on the machine.

![Cycle time estimate on the Summary step](/images/onboarding/screenshots/s57-summary-cycle-time-estimate.png)

## What the estimate includes

Cycle time combines two parts:

| Part | What it covers |
|------|----------------|
| **Machining time** | The cutting time for each pass and operation in the plan. |
| **Setup time** | Probing, setting the datum, and loading and unloading the part for each strategy. |

## Adjust setup time

Setup time is editable per strategy, so you can match your shop's real numbers. On the Summary step, open a strategy to find its **Setup times** fields and edit them (values are in seconds):

| Field | What it covers |
|-------|----------------|
| Load / Unload | Loading the part into the fixture and removing it afterward. |
| Datum Setup | Setting the work coordinate origin for the setup. |
| Probe Cycle | Probing the part to confirm its position. |

The pass breakdown below shows machining and non-cutting time per pass; machining time is not edited here.

<Tip>
Machining time comes from the plan's operations and is not edited here. To change it, adjust the upstream steps that drive cutting parameters, such as the tool recommendations.
</Tip>
