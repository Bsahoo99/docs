**NeuraChat** is an AI assistant you can ask about machining and about your work in Neuramill. It opens as a draggable chat panel and answers in plain text. NeuraChat is available in two places, and what it knows depends on where you open it:

| Where | What it knows |
|-------|---------------|
| In the **studio** (including review mode) | Your current session — the part, your setup, and your selections — plus the Neuramill knowledge base. |
| On the **Dashboard** | The Neuramill knowledge base only. It does not have a specific job's context. |

## Open NeuraChat

Click the **chat icon** in the sidebar — in the studio or on the Dashboard. The panel opens with "How can I help you?".

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the panel">
    Click the chat icon in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ask a question">
    Type your question and press **Enter** to send (use **Shift + Enter** for a new line).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Read the response">
    NeuraChat shows its reply as formatted text in the panel.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What you can do

- Ask questions about machining, and — in the studio — about your current part and plan.
- Scroll back through your message history, which is kept for the session.
- Clear the conversation with the **trash icon** at the top of the panel.
- Drag the panel by its top bar to reposition it, and close it with the **✕**.

<Note>
NeuraChat answers questions only — it does not change your plan. To edit a plan, use the studio steps. It is also available in [review mode](/review-mode/reviewing-a-completed-job), so you can ask about a completed session without changing it.
</Note>
