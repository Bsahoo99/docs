---
title: "Run the session completion checklist"
description: "Run through this checklist before calling a session done."
---
Use this checklist every time before you finalize a session.

## Before upload

<Check>STEP file is `.STEP` or `.STP` format (not `.STL`, `.OBJ`, or other)</Check>
<Check>STEP file contains only the workpiece — no reference planes, assembly components, or construction geometry</Check>
<Check>You know your stock dimensions (X × Y × Z)</Check>
<Check>You know the exact material alloy (not just the generic family)</Check>
<Check>GD&T drawing is located and in PDF or TIFF format (if you have one)</Check>

## GD&T review (only if you uploaded a drawing)

<Check>Extracted annotations reviewed for accuracy</Check>
<Check>Key tolerance callouts linked to the correct 3D features</Check>
<Check>GD&T Approved — Step 2 Direction of cut is now unlocked</Check>

## Step 1 — Machining Setup

<Check>Features approved at the top of the panel</Check>
<Check>Material selected — specific alloy, not just the generic family</Check>
<Check>Machine selected</Check>
<Check>Rigidity level set</Check>
<Check>Stock dimensions entered (X, Y, Z)</Check>
<Check>Green "Stock Validated" banner is showing</Check>
<Check>Machining Setup Approved — green badge is showing</Check>

## Step 2 — Direction of Cut

<Check>All features in the list have been reviewed</Check>
<Check>Each feature is assigned to the correct face direction</Check>
<Check>Incorrect features or features with wrong dimensions have been flagged</Check>
<Check>Features that should not be machined have been flagged as Extra Feature</Check>
<Check>Operations for each feature have been checked</Check>
<Check>Direction of Cut Approved — green badge is showing</Check>

## Step 3 — Clamping Strategy

<Check>At least one strategy added and approved</Check>
<Check>Feature Distribution Remaining counter = 0 (or all remaining features are intentionally excluded)</Check>
<Check>Operations within each strategy are in the correct order</Check>
<Check>No "Unsaved" indicator is showing on any strategy card</Check>
<Check>Complete Setup clicked — "Setup Completed" badge is showing</Check>

## Step 4 — Intelligent Tool Recommender

<Check>Select Optimal Tools clicked — "Tools Selected" badge is showing</Check>
<Check>Recommended tools reviewed for all operations</Check>
<Check>Speed and feed values noted or screenshotted for reference at the machine</Check>
<Check>Any manual overrides confirmed</Check>

## Step 5 — Summary

<Check>Summary reviewed — all operations, tools, and parameters look correct</Check>
<Check>Cycle time reviewed and setup times adjusted to your shop</Check>
<Check>"All Steps Complete" badge is visible in the footer</Check>
<Check>Session is saved on the Neuramill server</Check>

## Export to Fusion 360 (optional)

<Check>Export key generated from the completed job</Check>
<Check>CAM data imported into Fusion with the [Neuramill Import add-in](/fusion-addin/overview)</Check>
<Check>Setups, tools, and operations look correct in the Fusion CAM tree</Check>
