---
title: "Switch units"
description: "How to switch between millimeters and inches in the 3D viewer."
---
At the far right of the toolbar you will see two labels: **mm** and **in**.

- Click **mm** to show all measurements in millimeters
- Click **in** to show all measurements in inches

The currently active unit is highlighted (looks darker or underlined).

## Changes when you switch units

When you click mm or in, everything updates immediately:
- All dimension labels on the 3D model
- The Stock Size fields (X, Y, Z) in Machining Setup
- Any measurements shown by the Measure tool

<Warning>
If you switch units **after** you have already typed your stock dimensions, check the stock size fields again. The numbers convert automatically, but verify they look correct before continuing. Example: 305 mm becomes 12.0 in — if the conversion looks wrong, re-enter the value manually.
</Warning>
