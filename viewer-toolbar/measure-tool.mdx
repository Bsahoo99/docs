The **Measure** tool lets you click two points on the 3D model and see the exact distance between them. Useful for checking dimensions or comparing the model to your drawing.

## Use the Measure Tool

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click tool #3 to activate it">
    Click the **ruler/pencil icon** in the toolbar. The icon highlights to show it is active.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click your first point on the model">
    Move your mouse over the 3D model. The cursor changes shape.
    Click on the spot where you want to start the measurement.
    A small dot appears at that point.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click your second point">
    Move your mouse to the second point and click.
    A line appears between the two points and the distance is shown in a small label.
    
    {/* TODO: Add screenshot — Measure tool active — two points on the model with a line between them and a distance label */}
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear the measurement when done">
    To start a new measurement, click the **Reset View** button (tool #4) to clear it.
    Or click tool #3 again to deactivate the measure mode.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
The measurement reads in the unit system currently selected (mm or in). If you need a measurement in the other unit, switch using the mm/in switcher first, then remeasure.
</Tip>
