---
The Neuramill add-in is published on the **Autodesk App Store**. You install it from inside Fusion 360, then enable it so the **Neuramill Import** button appears in your Manufacturing workspace.

## Open the Fusion App Store

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Add-Ins menu">
    In Fusion 360, go to the **Utilities** tab and click the **ADD-INS** dropdown.

    {/* TODO: screenshot needed — Utilities tab with the ADD-INS dropdown open, showing Fusion App Store */}
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the Fusion App Store">
    Choose **Fusion App Store**. The store opens in your browser.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the Neuramill add-in">
    Search the store for the Neuramill add-in and open its listing. {/* TODO: add the Autodesk App Store listing URL */}
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download the installer for your operating system">
    Download the **macOS** or **Windows** installer to match your computer, then run it. Close Fusion 360 first so the installer can finish.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
The store has separate installers for **macOS** and **Windows**. Download the one that matches your computer.
</Note>

## Enable the add-in in Fusion

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Scripts and Add-Ins">
    Reopen Fusion 360, go to the **Utilities** tab, click **ADD-INS**, and choose **Scripts and Add-Ins** (or press `Shift` + `S`).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run it and turn on Run on Startup">
    On the **Add-Ins** tab, select the Neuramill add-in, click **Run**, and turn on **Run on Startup** so it loads automatically every time you open Fusion.

    {/* TODO: screenshot needed — Scripts and Add-Ins dialog with Neuramill add-in selected and Run on Startup checked */}
  </Step>
</Steps>

## If the add-in is blocked or will not install

Your operating system may block the installer because it comes from outside the system app store. Allow it, then restart Fusion 360.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="macOS — allow it in Privacy & Security">
    1. Open **System Settings** and go to **Privacy & Security**.
    2. Scroll to the message that the Neuramill installer was blocked, and click **Open Anyway** (also shown as **Allow Anyway**).
    3. Confirm with your password or Touch ID if prompted.
    4. Restart Fusion 360 and try again.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Windows — allow it through SmartScreen">
    1. If Windows shows an "unrecognized app" warning, click **More info**, then **Run anyway**.
    2. If the downloaded file is blocked, right-click it, choose **Properties**, tick **Unblock**, and click **OK**.
    3. Restart Fusion 360 and try again.

## Confirm it loaded

Switch to the **Manufacturing** workspace. You should see the **Neuramill Import** button in the toolbar. It also appears in the **ADD-INS** dropdown.

<Check>
The **Neuramill Import** button is visible in the Manufacturing workspace toolbar.
</Check>

If you still do not see the button, see the troubleshooting steps in [Importing CAM data](/fusion-addin/importing-cam-data).

<Note>
Need help installing the add-in? Contact the Neuramill team at founders@neuramill.co.
</Note>
