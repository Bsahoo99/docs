The Neuramill add-in for Fusion 360 turns a finished Neuramill plan into native Fusion CAM data. You get setups, tools, operations, and (optionally) generated toolpaths built directly in your Fusion document — ready to simulate, post-process, and run.

## The round trip

Your plan starts in the Neuramill web app and ends in Fusion 360. Three things connect them:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Finish a plan in the web app">
    Work through a session to a completed plan, ending at [Step 5 — Summary](/summary/summary-view). The export key is generated from the completed job.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate an export key">
    From the completed job, generate a one-time **export key** and copy it to your clipboard. See [Generating an export key](/fusion-addin/generating-an-export-key).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Import in Fusion 360">
    Open the **Neuramill Import** palette in Fusion, paste the key, and click **Import CAM Data**. The add-in builds the CAM data in your document. See [Importing CAM data](/fusion-addin/importing-cam-data).
  </Step>
</Steps>
