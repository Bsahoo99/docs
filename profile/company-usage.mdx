This section on your Profile page shows how much of your subscription plan your company has used so far.

![Company usage and limits with Sessions Users and Machines progress bars](/images/onboarding/screenshots/s14-company-usage-progress-bars.webp)

## Usage counters

On a **Trial plan**, you will see counters like these:

| Counter | Example | What It Means |
|---------|---------|---------------|
| Sessions Used | 3 / 5 | You have created 3 out of your 5 allowed sessions |
| Users | 1 / 1 | You are at your user limit — no more users can be added |
| Machines | 1 / 2 | You have set up 1 of your 2 allowed machines |

**Progress bars** show each counter visually:
- A **green** bar means you still have room.
- An **orange or red** bar means you are near or at the limit.

On an **Enterprise plan**, all counters show **"Unlimited."**

<Tip>
If any counter is at its limit and you need more, contact the Neuramill team about upgrading to Enterprise.
</Tip>
