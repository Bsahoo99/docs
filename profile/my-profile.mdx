Your Profile page shows your personal account information, your company's usage against its plan limits, and your Machining Policy Preferences.

## Open Your Profile

1. Look at the **top-right corner** of any Neuramill screen.
2. You will see a small circle with your initials inside it. This is your **profile icon**.
3. Click that circle once.
4. A small menu drops down. Click **My Profile**.

![Profile dropdown with My Profile highlighted](/images/onboarding/screenshots/s12-profile-menu-my-profile-highlight-duplicate.webp)

## Account Information

| Field | Can You Edit It? | What It Shows |
|-------|-----------------|---------------|
| Name | **Yes** — click the field to edit | Your first and last name |
| Email | No — contact admin to change | Your registered email address |
| Company | No — set by admin | Your company name |
| Role | No — assigned by admin | Super Admin, Admin, or User |
| Subscription Plan | No — managed by admin | Trial or Enterprise |

**To edit your name:**
1. Click inside the Name field.
2. Delete the existing name and type the new one.
3. Click **Save**.
