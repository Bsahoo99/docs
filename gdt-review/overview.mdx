When you start a session with a STEP file and a GD&T drawing, Neuramill extracts the drawing's annotations automatically and shows you the GD&T review screen. You see this screen before the studio steps so you can confirm the extracted dimensions, tolerances, and callouts and link them to the right features.

<Info>
You only see GD&T review if you uploaded a drawing. If your session has a STEP file alone, this section does not apply and you go straight into the studio steps. See [Upload CAD and GD&T](/starting-a-session/upload-cad-and-gdt) for how to start a session with a drawing.
</Info>

<Note>
**Approval gate:** Step 2 Direction of cut stays locked until you approve the GD&T annotations. Review and link your annotations, then use [Approve GD&T](/gdt-review/approving-gdt) to unlock the studio steps.
</Note>

## Why it matters

Linked tolerances feed the tool recommender and the speeds and feeds calculation. Tighter tolerances lead to tighter cutting parameters, so accurate annotations produce more accurate recommendations.

## The three parts of the screen

The GD&T review screen has three connected parts.

| Part | What it shows |
|------|---------------|
| Document viewer | Renders the uploaded `.pdf` or `.tiff` drawing. Zoom, pan, and page navigation. |
| Annotation markers | Numbered bubbles overlaid on the drawing, one per extracted annotation. |
| Annotation list | A table of every extracted annotation. Click a row to highlight its bubble, and click a bubble to highlight its row. |

Each annotation has a type, value, units, and tolerance.
