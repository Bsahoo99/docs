Approval is the sign-off that ends GD&T review. When a drawing was uploaded, Step 2 Direction of cut stays locked until you approve the annotations.

## What to check before approving

Confirm your review is complete before you approve.

- Every annotation matches the drawing in type, value, units, and tolerance. See [Reviewing annotations](/gdt-review/reviewing-annotations).
- Duplicate annotations are merged, and any incorrect ones are deleted.
- Each tolerance that matters is linked to the right feature. See [Linking annotations](/gdt-review/linking-annotations).
- The document-wide general tolerances match the drawing's title block.

## Approve GD&T

Use the **Approve GD&T** action to sign off the annotations. Approval unlocks Step 2 Direction of cut, and you proceed into the studio steps.

![Approve GD&T action on the GD&T review screen](/images/onboarding/screenshots/s46-gdt-approve-button.webp)

<Warning>
Linked tolerances feed the tool recommender and speeds and feeds. Approve only when the annotations are correct, because errors carry through to the recommendations in Step 4 Intelligent tool recommender.
</Warning>
