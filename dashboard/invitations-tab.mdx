The Invitations tab shows all invitations that have been sent out, along with their current status.

| Column | What It Means |
|--------|--------------|
| Email | The email address the invite was sent to |
| Role | What role they were invited as (Admin or User) |
| Status | **Pending** = not yet registered. **Accepted** = completed registration. |
| Sent | The date and time the invitation was sent |

## Resending an invitation

If someone says they did not receive their invite email, you can resend it from this tab. Find their row and click the **resend icon**.

<Note>
Invitation links expire after a short time. If the original link is expired, resend the invitation to generate a fresh link.
</Note>
