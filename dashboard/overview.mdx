After you sign in, you land on the **Dashboard**. This is your main home screen — the page you always come back to.

![Dashboard overview with all top tabs and New Session button highlighted](/images/onboarding/screenshots/s06-dashboard-overview-tabs-new-session.png)

## Dashboard tabs

At the top of the Dashboard you see a tab for each area. Click any tab to switch to it.

| Tab | What It Shows |
|-----|--------------|
| **Jobs** | Every machining session your team has created. This is where you start new jobs and reopen old ones. |
| **Machines** | The CNC machines configured for your company. |
| **Tools** | Your company's cutting tool library. |
| **Policies** | The machining policies that drive Neuramill's recommendations. |
| **Machining configuration** | The libraries that feed planning — materials, machines, workholdings, and tools. |
| **Users** | Your team members and their roles. (Admin and Super Admin only.) |
| **Invitations** | Pending and accepted invitations sent to new team members. (Admin and Super Admin only.) |
| **Usage** | How many jobs, machines, and users your company has. |

## + New session button

In the top-right corner of the Dashboard, you will always see a **+ New Session** button. Click it whenever you want to start planning a new part.
