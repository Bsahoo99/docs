The Users tab shows all team members who have a Neuramill account within your company.

<Note>
This tab is **only visible to Admins and Super Admins**. Regular Users do not see it.
</Note>

## Users list details

| Column | What It Shows |
|--------|--------------|
| Name | The team member's full name |
| Email | Their registered email address |
| Role | Super Admin, Admin, or User |
| Status | **Active** = they have completed registration. **Pending** = they were invited but have not registered yet. |

## Invite a new team member

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click the Invite User button">
    On the Users tab, find and click the **Invite User** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in the invitation form">
    Enter the new person's email address and select their role: **Admin** or **User**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Send Invite">
    Click **Send Invite**. An email is sent to that address with a registration link.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for them to register">
    Until they click the link and complete registration, they appear in the list with a **Pending** status.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Delete a user

1. Find the user in the list.
2. Click the **delete icon** (or the three-dot menu) next to their name.
3. A confirmation message appears. Click **Confirm** to delete.

<Warning>
Deleting a user removes their access immediately. Their past sessions are **not** deleted — they remain visible in the Jobs tab.
</Warning>
