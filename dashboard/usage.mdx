The **Usage** view shows your company's account usage against its plan limits. It tracks three counters — jobs, machines, and users — each with a progress bar so you can see how much of your plan you have used.

## Usage counters

| Counter | What it shows |
|---------|---------------|
| Jobs | Jobs used against the maximum your plan allows. |
| Machines | Machines configured against the maximum your plan allows. |
| Users | Team members against the maximum your plan allows. |

Each counter has a **progress bar** that fills as you approach the limit.

<Tip>
If a counter is at its limit and you need more, contact the Neuramill team about upgrading your plan.
</Tip>

## Where else to find usage

The same usage figures appear in your Profile area. The Profile view is the place to check usage outside the Dashboard.
