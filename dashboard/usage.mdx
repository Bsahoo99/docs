The **Usage** view shows your company's current usage at a glance — how many jobs you have run, and how many machines and users are set up.

![Usage view with jobs, machines, and users counts](/images/onboarding/screenshots/s70-dashboard-usage-counts.webp)

## Usage counters

| Counter | What it shows |
|---------|---------------|
| Jobs | The number of jobs your team has created. |
| Machines | The number of machines configured for your company. |
| Users | The number of team members on your account. |

<Note>
Your account does not have usage limits — these counters are for visibility, not caps.
</Note>
