After approving a strategy, a **strategy card** appears. Click it to expand and see the full list of **machining operations** (called passes) for that setup.

![Expanded strategy card with numbered operation list](/images/onboarding/screenshots/s40-clamping-expanded-operation-list.png)

## Operation meanings

| Operation | What It Is in Plain Language |
|-----------|------------------------------|
| **#1 FACING** | Cuts the top surface of the stock flat. **This is almost always the first operation** — it creates a clean, flat reference surface. Every machinist knows: face it first. |
| **#2 ROUGHING** | Removes the bulk of the material quickly. Heavy cuts, not worrying about surface finish yet. Leaves a thin layer of material for the finishing pass. |
| **#3+ FINISHING** | Light passes to achieve final dimensions and surface finish. There may be multiple finishing passes for different surfaces. |
| **SPOT DRILLING** | Creates a small center-point mark before drilling. Prevents the drill from walking (drifting) off the intended hole center. |
| **DRILLING** | Creates the hole using a rotating drill bit. |
| **REAMING** | A precision pass inside a drilled hole to hit a very tight diameter tolerance. Only appears if reaming was enabled in the Direction of Cut step. |

## Strategy card header

The collapsed strategy card header shows:

| Element | What It Shows |
|---------|--------------|
| Strategy number | Auto-named: Strategy 1, Strategy 2, etc. |
| Pass count | How many operations are in this setup |
| Feature count | How many features are covered |
| Workholding | The workholding device you selected |
| Face badges | Red = Datum, Orange = Blocked, Green = Accessible |
