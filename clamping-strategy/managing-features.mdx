---
title: "Manage features in operations"
description: "How to add or remove features from operations, and how to reorder operations within a strategy."
---
After approving a strategy, you can adjust which features belong to which operation, and change the order of operations.

## Adding a Feature to an Operation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Expand the strategy card">
    Click the strategy card to expand it and see the operation list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the operation you want to add to">
    Locate the operation row (e.g., #3 FINISHING).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click the + button on that operation row">
    On the right side of the operation row, click the **+** button.
    A search panel opens on the right side of the screen with a list of available features.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the feature you want to add">
    Hover your mouse over any feature in the list — the 3D viewer highlights that feature in blue so you can confirm it is the right one.
    
    ![Feature add panel with checkboxes and highlighted feature](/images/onboarding/screenshots/s41-clamping-add-feature-panel.webp)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check the checkbox and click Add">
    Click the checkbox next to the feature(s) you want to add.
    Click the **Add X Features** button at the bottom of the panel.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Removing a Feature from an Operation

1. Expand the strategy card.
2. Click the operation row to expand its feature list.
3. Each feature appears as a small card. Click the **X** on that card to remove it.
4. The feature goes back to the unassigned pool (the Remaining counter increases by 1).

## Reordering Operations

The standard order is: **Facing → Roughing → Finishing → Drilling**. But if your part needs a different sequence, you can drag operations to reorder them.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Expand the strategy card">
    Click the card to see the operation list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the drag handle">
    On the left side of each operation row, you will see a **drag handle** — it looks like six small dots (⠿) or three short horizontal lines.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click, hold, and drag">
    Click and hold the drag handle. While holding, drag the operation up or down to the new position. Release to drop it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save immediately">
    As soon as you release, the words **"Unsaved"** appear at the top of the strategy card.
    
    **Click the Save icon** (looks like a floppy disk 💾) immediately. **If you do not save, your reordering will be lost when you click away.**
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
**The most common mistake in this step is forgetting to save after reordering.** The "Unsaved" text is there specifically to remind you. Do not ignore it. Click Save before clicking anything else.
</Warning>
