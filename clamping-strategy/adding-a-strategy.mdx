---
title: "Add a clamping strategy"
description: "How to fill out the New Clamping Strategy form — datum plane, fixture type, blocked faces, and approving."
---
Each strategy represents one physical clamping setup — one time the part is in the fixture.

## Step 1: Click + Add Clamping Strategy

On the Clamping Strategy screen, click the **+ Add Clamping Strategy** button. It appears as a dashed-border card in the strategy area.

A form opens: **New Clamping Strategy**

![New clamping strategy form with all controls visible](/images/onboarding/screenshots/s37-clamping-new-strategy-form.webp)

## Step 2: Choose the Datum Plane

The **Datum Plane** is the face of the part that sits down on the fixture. It is your reference face for this setup — the face that touches the vise jaw, the fixture plate, or the parallels.

1. Click the **Datum Plane** dropdown.
2. Choose the face: Top, Bottom, Front, Back, Left, or Right.

**Example:** If you are laying the part down with the bottom face on the vise parallels, choose **Bottom**.

## Step 3: Choose the Fixture Type

1. Click the **Fixture Type** dropdown.
2. Choose the workholding device you are using.

Examples: Talon-grip / step-jaw vise, standard soft-jaw vise, dovetail fixture, vacuum chuck, strap clamps.

## Step 4: Block the Covered Faces

This is the most important part of the form. You are telling Neuramill which faces of the part **the tool cannot reach** because the fixture is in the way.

There are **six face buttons**: Top, Bottom, Front, Back, Left, Right.

| Button Color | What It Means |
|-------------|---------------|
| **Red** | Datum face — the part is sitting on this face. The tool cannot reach it from outside. (Set automatically when you choose the Datum Plane.) |
| **Orange** | Blocked — the fixture jaw or clamp is touching this face. The tool cannot reach it. |
| **Green** | Accessible — the tool can reach this face in this setup. |

**To block a face:** Click its button. It turns orange.
**To un-block a face:** Click its orange button again. It turns green.

As you click, a **summary line** at the bottom of the section updates in real time:
*"Datum: Bottom | Blocked: Left, Right | Accessible: Top, Front, Back"*

![Clamping face button states for datum blocked and accessible](/images/onboarding/screenshots/s38-clamping-face-buttons-color-states.webp)

<Tip>
**Double-click any face button** to highlight that face on the 3D viewer. Use this to visually confirm which faces your fixture is actually touching before you click.
</Tip>

## Step 5: Add Notes (Optional)

Type any reminders in the **Notes** field. For example:
- *"6-inch Kurt vise, 0.5-inch step jaws"*
- *"Part sits on parallels, top face accessible"*

These notes appear on the strategy card and in the Summary.

## Step 6: Approve the Strategy

1. Read the summary line one more time and confirm the blocked faces are correct.
2. Click the **Approve Strategy** button.
3. The form closes and a **strategy card** appears on the screen.
