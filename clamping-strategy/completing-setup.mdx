## Before you complete — quick checklist

<Check>At least one strategy has been approved</Check>
<Check>The Remaining counter shows 0 (or you have deliberately accepted that some features cannot be reached)</Check>
<Check>Operations within each strategy are in the correct order</Check>
<Check>No "Unsaved" indicator is showing on any strategy card</Check>

## Complete

1. Check the **Remaining counter** — confirm it is 0, or as low as your part geometry allows.
2. Click the **Complete Setup** button at the top of the Clamping Strategy section.
3. The button changes to a green **✓ Setup Completed** badge.
4. The **Intelligent Tool Recommender** step unlocks in the navigation bar.
